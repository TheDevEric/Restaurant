FAQ

How do you reserve a table

To reserve a table at Zheng's you must call us 1 week prior.

How many chefs do you have

We have 5 chefs. Here at Zheng's we believe the key thing in a restaurant is the quality of the chef. Chef Zheng hand picks and trains the chefs.

What is the best dish at Zheng's

Here at Zheng's we do a 6 course meal, and it varies every week, but the dish that we got the best feedback from is the peking Duck. It is marinated, cooked, sliced, and the whole process takes around 7 hours.

What are your hours

We open only for dinner services throughout the week at 5pm-11pm.
