Our Restaurant

Chef Zheng has been our head chef since the restaurant opened. He is originally from Beijing, but he decided to come to United States to further enhance his culinary skills. He started the restaurant: Zheng's to let the people of America taste authentic Chinese cuisine. Since the opening of Zheng's he has gotten 3 michelin stars, and continues to improve his techniques.

We use the best and the freshest ingredients to further elevate our food. We always follow our motto fresh never frozen, and we still stand by that. We get all of our produce from the Bob's Farm. Thanks to them we provide our customers with the best non gmo food, cage free chicken, and freshly picked vegetables
